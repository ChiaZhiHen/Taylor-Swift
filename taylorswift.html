Taylor Swift (2008)

Genres: Country

Taylor Swift's debut album peaked at #5 on Billboard 200. It had an incredible longevity on the chart, charted for more than 250 weeks

Fearless (Taylor's Version) (2021)

Genres: Country

The original version was released in 2008 and called "Fearless". Fearless won Taylor her first ever "Album of The Year" at the 52nd GRAMMY Awards.
After the master incident, she re-recored this album and re-released it in 2021.

Speak Now (2010)

Genres: Country

It was her first album that sold over 1million copies in its first week. All songs in this album were self-written.

RED (2012)

Genres: Country

Taylor Swift got her first #1 hit on Hot 100 with the lead single of this album, which is "We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together"
It spawned several iconic hits of her career, including "I Knew You Were Trouble" and "22"

reputation (2017)

Genres: Pop

My favorite album of all times. This is her first release after the KimYe drama.
It showed a different side of Taylor, having a much darker sounds and images.Taylor didn't do any interview for the "reputation" era.

Lover (2019)

Genres: Pop

This is her most mature pop album till date. Discussing on the topic of love, self-positivity, women empowerment and more.
The lyrics reflected how sweet her life of with her lover Joe Alywn.
She also becomes more active on interview shows in this era.

folklore (2020)

Genres: Alternative

A masterpiece released in the middle of the pandemic. This is the first album that Taylor didn't do a proper rollout before the album releases.
It was surprise announced on 23 July 2020,and it shocked the whole internet. Released on the next day of the announcement, this album broke multiple records.
"folklore" proved us that Taylor's power is still unstoppable even more than 10 years into her career.
It won Taylor her third ever "Album of The Year" at the 63rd GRAMMY Awards, made her the first and ever woman to win "Album of The Year" thrice.
She is also the only artist to win "AOTY" three times with three different genres

  • Fearless (Country)
  • 1989 (Pop)
  • folklore (Alternative)

evermore (2020)

Genres: Alternative

The sister album of "folklore", another surprise release of Taylor. It continued the beautiful stories of "folklore".
